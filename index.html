About

Karachi Tarka is a family-operated kitchen that brings the heart of Karachi's culinary traditions to Springfield, VA, United States, Virginia. This endeavor is the brainchild of Mr. Aurangzeb and Mrs. Khurshida, who, along with their son Asad and daughter-in-law Faryal, have dedicated themselves to preserving and sharing the authentic flavors of their hometown. Having migrated from Karachi to Springfield, VA, the family understands the longing for home-cooked meals that evoke the essence of Karachi's vibrant street food culture. They are committed to providing services to families and immigrants who crave the authentic Karachi cuisine they remember and love. At Karachi Tarka, you can expect signature recipes cooked the traditional way, offering a genuine taste of Karachi right here in Springfield, VA, United States, Virginia.
